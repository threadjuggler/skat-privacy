1. Data We Collect

Skat is a card game played against computer-controlled opponents. The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. No registration or user account is required. Game progress, statistics, and settings are kept temporarily on your device only.

However, the App integrates third-party services that may collect certain data:

Google AdMob (Advertising)

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob (banner ads and occasional full-screen ads between games) to fund its free operation. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location data (country/region level)
  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

The legal basis is your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) for personalized advertising, or our legitimate interest in funding the free offering (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) for non-personalized advertising. If you have purchased the "Ad-free" in-app purchase, no advertisements are loaded or displayed anymore.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

"Ad-free" In-App Purchase (Google Play / Apple App Store)

The App offers an optional one-time in-app purchase that permanently removes advertising. The purchase is processed entirely by the app store through which you installed the App (Google Play or Apple App Store):

  • Payment is processed exclusively by the respective store. We do not receive or store any payment details, names, or addresses.
  • The App merely queries the store to check whether the purchase exists (including when restoring the purchase on a new device). The purchase record remains with the store; we do not store it on servers of our own.

The legal basis is the performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR). The processing of your payment data is subject to the privacy policy of the respective store operator: Google or Apple.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

The third-party data processing described above serves the following purposes:

  • Advertising — to display ads within the App and fund the free offering (AdMob)
  • Purchase processing — to process the optional "Ad-free" in-app purchase and to check whether the purchase exists (Google Play / Apple App Store)

3. Consent for Personalized Ads (EU/EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, the App will request your consent on first launch before showing personalized advertisements, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This prompt is presented through the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP). You may choose to:

  • Consent to personalized ads based on your interests
  • Decline personalized ads — you will still see ads, but they will not be tailored to your interests

You can change your consent choice at any time: via the "Datenschutz" (Privacy) menu item in the App, or by clearing the App's data in your device settings, which will trigger a new consent prompt.

After purchasing the "Ad-free" option, the App no longer displays any advertising; consent for personalized ads then no longer applies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared with the following third parties solely for the purposes described above:

  • Google LLC / Google Ireland Ltd. — for advertising (AdMob) and for purchase processing via Google Play
  • Apple Inc. / Apple Distribution International Ltd. — for purchase processing via the Apple App Store

5. Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. Data collected by the third-party services (Google, Apple) — including the purchase record for the in-app purchase — is subject to their respective retention policies.

Game progress, statistics, and settings are kept in memory only and discarded when you close the App; they are not stored permanently.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the EU/EEA, you have the following rights under the GDPR:

  • Right of access — request information about what data is processed
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Right to restriction — request restriction of data processing
  • Right to object — object to data processing
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw your consent for personalized ads at any time
  • Right to lodge a complaint — with a supervisory authority in your member state

To exercise any of these rights regarding data collected by Google, please refer to Google's Privacy Controls. To exercise rights regarding this App, contact us using the information below.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided data through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
